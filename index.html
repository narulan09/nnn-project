Plot

Eren is best characterized by such definitions as determination, perseverance, impulsiveness. Since childhood, Eren dreamed of joining the Survey Corps and going beyond the walls. He compared the life of people in the Walls to the life of cattle in a cage.

Eren always cared about his childhood best friend, Armin, before he met Mikasa and his family, he was willing to sacrifice his life for them. This was most often demonstrated in Eren's attempts to protect Armin from street bullies who were significantly stronger than both of them combined, as well as when he tried to pick up the rubble of a house that fell on his mother in an attempt to save her life during the fall of Shiganshina. Eren is also capable of being especially brutal in protecting people he doesn't even know, but who he believes really need help.This was demonstrated when Eren killed the two kidnappers who stabbed Mikasa's parents to death. After the death of his mother at the hands of a titan, Eren lost contact with his father and even became angry with him because he did not have time to return and help save Carla. Since Eren values ​​his comrades very much, he becomes enraged when he realizes that they are in danger.

Biography

Eren Yeager (Japanese: エレン・イェーガー, Hepburn: Eren Yēgā), also spelled Eren Jaeger (Turkish: Eren, "Saint"; German: Jaeger/Jäger, "Hunter"), is the protagonist of the Attack on Titan manga series created by Hajime Isayama. Eren is a teenager who swears revenge on enormous man-eating humanoid creatures known as Titans, who have forced what remains of the human race to live in walled off cities and devoured his mother while destroying his home town in the Shiganshina district of Wall Maria. In order to defeat the Titans, Eren enlists in the Military and joins the Survey Corps—an elite group of soldiers who fight Titans outside the walls and also study the physiology of Titans in order to know what they are fighting. As the story progresses, Eren gains the power of becoming a Titan later identified as "Attack Titan" (進撃の巨人, Shingeki no Kyojin). He has also appeared in other related media including anime and video games.

Isayama created Eren with the idea of a character whose fears and dreams were relatable but often clashed with his own darkness, resulting into multiple changes to his characterization. In the anime adaptation of the series, Eren has been voiced by Yūki Kaji in Japanese and Bryce Papenbrook in English. Both of these actors found difficulties in employing different types of voices based on how Eren grows up across the narrative. In the live-action film adaptations, he is portrayed by Haruma Miura.

Critical reception to Eren was initially polarized, finding him too antagonistic and harsh for his age. Positive comments focused on the character's ideals and newfound powers as a Titan as well as his character arc. Reception has become more positive in later arcs of the manga and anime as he becomes a more ambiguous rather than heroic character, evidenced in the 6th Crunchyroll Anime Awards where Eren was nominated for "Best Protagonist" and "Best Antagonist" simultaneously, and went on to win in the latter category. The character has proven popular within the Attack on Titan fanbase, while his voice actors Kaji and Papenbrook received praise for their portrayals of the character.

Main characters

Armin Arlert

Armin Arlert starts as a timid, introspective boy but evolves into a brilliant strategist whose intellect and compassion are crucial to the team. His keen analytical mind and moral integrity drive the group’s tactics and decisions, helping to navigate through crises with a mix of strategic genius and empathy. Despite his initial lack of confidence, Armin's courage and thoughtful leadership become pivotal in their fight for freedom and survival. His growth is marked by his ability to balance his deep empathy with the harsh realities of their world, making him an invaluable asset to his friends and allies.

Eren Yeager

Eren Yeager is a fiercely determined and often impulsive character, driven by a deep desire for freedom and vengeance. His journey from a passionate youth who vows to eradicate the Titans to a complex, morally ambiguous figure reflects both his relentless drive and the heavy burdens of his mission. As he uncovers the truth about his powers and the world, Eren's actions become increasingly controversial, challenging his friends and redefining his role in the ongoing conflict. His transformation highlights the tension between idealism and reality, making him a central and polarizing force in the struggle for humanity's survival.

Mikasa Ackerman

Mikasa Ackerman is an extraordinarily skilled and protective soldier, known for her unmatched combat prowess and fierce loyalty to Eren Yeager. Orphaned and traumatized at a young age, her adoptive bond with Eren fuels her unyielding dedication to his safety and the group's well-being. Her combat abilities and unbreakable resolve make her a formidable warrior, while her emotional depth and personal struggles add layers to her character. Mikasa’s journey is marked by her struggle to reconcile her protective instincts with the harsh realities of war, highlighting her inner strength and the complexity of her relationships with those she loves.
